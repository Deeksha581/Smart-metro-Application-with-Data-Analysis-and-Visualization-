Smart Metro Travel & Analytics System

The Smart Metro Travel & Analytics System is a web-based application designed to enhance urban public transportation by integrating metro services with intelligent data analytics.

It helps commuters plan efficient journeys while enabling metro authorities to analyze passenger flow, station usage, and peak-hour trends for improved operational efficiency.

🚉 Route & Station Information

Accurate metro routes, stations, and connectivity details.

📊 Passenger Flow Analytics

Insights into passenger movement and demand patterns.

⏱ Peak Hour Insights

Identifies busiest stations and optimal travel times.

🧠 Smart Metro Services

Data-driven decision support for efficient metro operations.
