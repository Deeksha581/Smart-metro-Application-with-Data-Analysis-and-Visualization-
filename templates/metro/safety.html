Safety Measures in Bangalore Metro

Ensuring a safe, secure and comfortable journey for every passenger

Overview

Safety is a top priority in Bangalore Metro (Namma Metro). Multiple security systems, trained personnel, and strict passenger guidelines are implemented to ensure a secure travel environment. These measures help in preventing accidents, controlling crowds, and responding quickly during emergencies.

📹 CCTV Surveillance

All metro stations, platforms, entry points and coaches are under continuous CCTV surveillance. This helps in monitoring passenger movement, preventing crimes, and enabling quick action during emergencies.

🚺 Women-Only Coaches

Dedicated women-only coaches are available, especially during peak hours. This initiative ensures safety, comfort and dignity for women commuters.

📞 Emergency Helpline

Emergency helpline numbers are displayed inside metro trains and stations. Passengers can immediately contact authorities to report emergencies or suspicious activities.

👮 Security Staff at Stations

Trained CISF and metro security personnel are deployed at all stations and inside trains to assist passengers, manage crowds and handle emergency situations efficiently.

Safety Rules & Passenger Awareness

Passenger Guidelines

  • Follow station announcements and safety instructions
  • Do not carry prohibited or dangerous items
  • Respect women-only coach regulations
  • Maintain social distancing during peak hours
  • Report unattended baggage immediately
  • Stand behind the yellow safety line on platforms
  • Offer priority seating to senior citizens and pregnant women
