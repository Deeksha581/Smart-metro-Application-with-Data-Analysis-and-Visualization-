🪪 Metro Pass System

Best option for daily & regular Bangalore Metro travellers

Why Choose a Metro Pass?

Metro passes are designed for passengers who travel frequently. They offer unlimited travel for a fixed duration, saving money, time and avoiding daily ticket purchases.

  • Unlimited metro travel within validity period
  • No need to purchase token tickets daily
  • Faster entry using smart card scan
  • Cost-effective for office commuters & students

🗓️ Monthly Pass

Unlimited travel for 30 days

₹ 1,200
  • Valid for 30 days from activation
  • Best for daily office commuters
  • Unlimited rides on all metro lines
  • Maximum savings compared to tokens

Important Rules & Notes

  • Pass is valid only for the registered user
  • First-time smart card must be collected at metro counter
  • Pass cannot be transferred to another person
  • Lost cards must be reported immediately (demo note)
  • Pass validity starts from activation date
